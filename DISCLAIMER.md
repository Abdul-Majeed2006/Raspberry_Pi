# DISCLAIMER & SAFETY NOTICE

## Hardware Safety

Working with electronics involves real physical risks.

- **Electrical Shorts:** Incorrect wiring can permanently damage your Raspberry Pi, sensors, or computer USB ports.
- **Heat:** Components can become extremely hot if wired incorrectly.
- **Batteries:** Lithium batteries (LiPo/Li-Ion) can catch fire if punctured, shorted, or overcharged.

**Always double-check your wiring before applying power.**
